Harriot Menus Editing Index & Instructions

Instructions:

  1. Ensure you are logged into your Conferre Group email account (@harriotmelbourne.com, @conferregroup.com etc) in your browser — you can log in at https://accounts.google.com/signin
  2. Click the link for the menu you want to edit – this will open the sheet in view-only mode if you don’t already have edit access.
  3. Click the blue “View only” button at the top toolbar of the sheet.
  4. In the popup that appears, click the green “Request edit access” button.
  5. Wait for confirmation that your request has been approved before making any changes.
  6. Once you’ve edited the spreadsheet, wait 5–15 seconds and reload the Harriot website to see your updates reflected online.
